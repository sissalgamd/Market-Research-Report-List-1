<p><p>Hoists are categorized into four main market types: </p><p>1. **Manual Hoists** utilize human power to lift loads, often favored for their simplicity and cost-effectiveness.</p><p>2. **Electric Hoists** use electric motors for lifting, providing efficiency and ease of use in industrial applications.</p><p>3. **Air Hoists** operate on compressed air, suitable for environments where electricity poses a risk, offering high lifting capabilities.</p><p>4. **Hydraulic Hoists** leverage hydraulic pressure for lifting heavy loads, known for their strength and precision in construction and heavy-duty operations.</p></p>
