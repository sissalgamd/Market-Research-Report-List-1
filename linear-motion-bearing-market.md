<p><ul><li>Rolling Bearing</li><li>Plain Bearing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The linear motion bearing market consists of two primary types: rolling bearings and plain bearings. Rolling bearings utilize elements like balls or rollers to reduce friction, providing precise movement and supporting higher loads, making them ideal for applications requiring speed and accuracy. In contrast, plain bearings operate on a sliding motion, relying on a smooth surface to minimize contact friction, typically offering simplicity and durability in lower-load applications. Both types cater to diverse industries, enhancing machinery efficiency and performance.</p></p>
