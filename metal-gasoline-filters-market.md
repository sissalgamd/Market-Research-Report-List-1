<p><strong>The Metal Gasoline Filters Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>In-Line Type</li><li>Element/Cartridge Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The metal gasoline filters market is primarily categorized into two types: In-Line Type and Element/Cartridge Type. In-Line Type filters are installed directly in the fuel line, providing efficient filtration by removing contaminants before they reach the engine. They typically feature a compact design for easy installation. Element/Cartridge Type filters, on the other hand, consist of a replaceable filter element housed in a metal casing, offering enhanced filtration performance. Both types are vital for maintaining engine performance and prolonging the lifespan of fuel systems.</p></p>
