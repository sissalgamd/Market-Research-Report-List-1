<p><p>The Smart Photovoltaic Street Light market features two primary types based on control technology: those utilizing single chip microcomputers and those employing comparators. Street lights with single chip microcomputers offer advanced control and processing capabilities, enabling features like adaptive brightness and remote monitoring. In contrast, comparator-based systems are simpler, providing basic functionality by comparing light levels to trigger the lighting system. Both types enhance energy efficiency and sustainability in urban lighting by leveraging solar power for illumination.</p></p>
